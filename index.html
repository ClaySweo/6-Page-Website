The History of Graphics Cards and CPUs


CPUs and GPUs are very important parts of a full computer. Even though a dedicated GPU is not vital to a working computer, they are still needed for gaming and rendering. The CPU is the brain of the computer, doing all of the real work and computation. It is made up of a system of threads and cores, which each do calculations seperately yet work together. The GPU creates the images that you see on a monitor. These two work together, along with the other components in the computer, to create a full system.
